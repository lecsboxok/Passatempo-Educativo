Accessible Culture

The Special Leisure Project - Accessible Culture has been carried out since 2011, enabling people with disabilities linked to rehabilitation centers to participate in the cultural circuit in an accessible way, offering the conditions to stimulate their potential and interests.

Cultural workshops and visits to museums, parks, exhibitions, theatrical sketches through the development of perceptive/sensory aspects as resources available in musicalization, in the arts, in living.








Make monitored educational visits accessible to cultural routes such as museums, exhibitions, concerts, plays, musicals, parks with special accompaniment for the disabled public, with personalized preparation selected according to the profile of the group of visitors, the theme of the event and the accessibility of the site.
The action is preceded by a preparation workshop that integrates a playful, exploratory, multisensory experience, body dynamics and conceptual aspects in order to stimulate the assimilation of the contents.
