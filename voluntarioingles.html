ROUANET LAW

Allows the sponsoring entrepreneur to discount up to 100% of the invested value amount invested in the project, from income tax due, as long as it does not exceed the permitted rate with the limit of 4% of the Income Tax due by Legal Entities and 6% by Individuals.



E.g.: If a company pays R$ 10 million in income tax to the government, it can allocate therefore, R$ 400 thousand to and sponsor a cultural project, obtaining the exhibition counterparts of a normal sponsorship. normal sponsorship. This amount will come as a deduction or rebate in the following year's income tax.


INCENTIVE LAW

TO SPORTS

Companies can allocate 1% of the income tax due to projects approved by the in the Federal Law of Incentive to Sports (Law 11.438/06). The operation of this law is very similar to the operation of the federal law of incentive to culture.

CULTURE OF SÃO PAULO

PROAC is a program established by the State Law of Incentive to Culture (Law 12.268/06). It allows the sponsor of projects approved by PROAC to have the right to of the value invested in the project, in ICMS, respecting a base of percentages that goes from 0.038 of the 0.038 of the ICMS due (for companies that pay tax equal to or higher than R$ 4 billion) to 3% of the ICMS (for companies that pay tax equal to or less than R$ 74,999,999.99).


FUMCAD

Based on the Municipal Fund for the Rights of Children and Adolescents 8.069/90 and Municipal Law 11.247/92. The project has to be approved by the Municipal Council for the Rights of the Child and Adolescent of São Paulo. Who can donate Legal entity: can direct up to 1% of the amount of income tax calculated based on real profit to to the FUMCAD and have this amount deducted in the payment of the tax. Individuals: the limit for the destination of the refund is up to 6% and is only possible for those who use the complete Annual Adjustment Declaration form.
