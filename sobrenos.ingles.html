Who we are

Passatempo Educativo is an OSCIP (Civil Society Organization of Public Interest) that has been operating for over 23 years in various projects in order to help in the social and educational development of children.

GOAL

Our main objective is to help in the teaching-learning process, with directly educational projects, which help to reduce inequality in access to education and provide this very important means to the poorest and neediest.

HAPPY COMPANY

We start from the assumption that a company that cares about the well-being of its employees results in a happy and motivated team, increasing its results and reaching its goals.

History






The first work team had only three people, and the initiative for work in this area of ​​educational leisure came from physical education teacher Carla Ravaioli and Carlos Chinen

After many months of research and work in schools in São Paulo, the projects gained a social dimension.

Our first activity was a Christmas Party for 150 children in December at the Colmeia Institution, currently known as the Ir.Maria Bernadette Educational Center, located in the northern part of the city of São Paulo. Since that day, educational activities have been carried out for children in this place.

The Maria Bernadette Center is an institution maintained by the Congregation of the Sisters of São José de Chambéry that serves 300 children and adolescents from tenements and slums in the northern region of São Paulo. Even today, one of our projects takes place in this same institution.

Meet our team

The team is made up of professionals from the areas of Leisure and Tourism, Recreation and Events, Physical Education, Pedagogy, Biology, Psychology, Journalism, Plastic Arts, Theater and collaborating professors.

Passatempo Educativo is aware that the values ​​defended by a monitor are very important not only for there to be a relationship of respect and successful work, but, above all, for there to be a positive and healthy exchange of experiences and knowledge among all employees. involved during each project.

Passatempo Educativo is a non-governmental, non-profit organization that develops projects in the educational, cultural, environmental preservation and sustainable tourism development areas.
