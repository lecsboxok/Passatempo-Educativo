Soccer Science

It is a project that allies soccer practice and learning, through its interfaces with biology, physics, and history (areas of knowledge that are present in the teaching curriculum of children and teenagers and that can be better assimilated through the continuity of teaching-learning, having soccer as a strategy and support).








It encourages the project's participants to develop their motor, cognitive, and affective-social skills critically and proactively, besides promoting the rescue of the history of soccer, Brazil, and the world, contributing to the education of young people to act as volunteers in sporting events with notions of tourism and hospitality.
